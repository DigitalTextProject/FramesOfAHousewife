1. Theoretical background and Research questions

The idea of creating a Digital Scholarly Edition of Birth and death of the Housewife was born and supported by some analogies that we intercepted between the nature of the novel and the digital space.

As Lucia Re argues, Paola Masino’s work is a polyphonic novel, in the way the author, “proprio per evadere e confrontarsi con la censura, ma mantenendo allo stesso tempo un profondo impegno critico nei confronti del fascismo, Masino costruisce un discorso basato su una pluralità dei piani narrativi e delle voci che il censore è costituzionalmente incapace di decifrare, proprio perché solitamente egli si limita a una lettura monologica e letterale del testo”.

This process resembles a lot the digital practice of editing in a digital paradigm whose collaborative and fluid nature creates a crack into the traditional idea of authority and authoritative text in philology: The edition invites the team of editors and collaborators to add more material and more knowledge. As regards the people involved and the roles they play, the road leads from the single omniscient editor of the printed edition to the team of specialists with differentiated roles in the conceptually and technically complex digital edition. It leads to a social edition, where input comes from within the team and from outside

Moreover, considering the notion of editorialization conceptualized by Vitali-Rosati a digital space can be said to be also informed by the dialogue of culture and technology, as “editorialization also describes the ways our cultural traditions influence our ways of structuring content”.

In other words, one could argue that digital editing is itself a polyphonic discourse, were the final outcome is a “patchwork” of interpretations and perspectives and, by means of the hyperlinked structure, can be experienced in a non-linear unexpected form.

The same goes for our novel, since the author herself states to have embraced the absurd features that came out of this process of fragmentations and recompositions as some unforeseen but “tasty” peculiarities of the narrative. Almost as if the body of the novel and of the character portrayed in it, trying to escape the judgement of the censor as well as the historical drawbacks, ended up escaping the authorial control too and developed an independent life of their own: Ripreso dopo sei anni, per desiderio dell’editore, il romanzo, tentai, sulle bozze di stampa che m’erano rimaste, di riportarlo alla prima edizione; ma non potrei giurare che in qualche punto lezione vecchia e lezione nuova non si mescolino. Non ho voluto accanirmi troppo a rimettere a posto ogni cosa; se ne è nato qualche assurdo, chi sa non s’intoni fatalmente e saporitamente con le altre originarie assurdità di questo ritratto di donna;

According to this theoretical framework we formulated the following research questions as a starting point for our work:

  1. How did the dialogue between the censor and the author unfold? What texts where the result of this process?
  2. How can the digital environment enhance the polyphonic structure of the novel?

2. Chosen sample of the novel and our interpretation

We chose to encode as a sample of the Novel, the beginning of Chapter 6. This is the central chapter of the novel and the longest one, in which the protagonist is alone in the Capital city, portrayed for the first and only time in an independent-like condition. The Housewife seems to undertake a more introspective path ant this is also suggested by the narrative, which takes the form of an intimate journal. Moreover, this chapter was one of the most incriminated by the censorship ate the times, as we can learn to Paola Masino correspondence with the editor. For these reasons it seemed to us the most suitable section of the novel to encode.

From the analysis of the variant readings here is what we were able to highlight:

We noted that different blocks of text were moved from their original place or expanded. It’s the case of the Journal entries of the Housewife, that we know had to be rearranged in the first edition due to editorial directions:
Le Memorie della Massaia non sono pubblicabili in un giornale, mentre potranno benissimo figurare nel volume. Anche il brano gustosissimo che segue il diario (il vagabondare della Massaia prima di trovare la ragazza) è troppo ermetico per il pubblico di “Tempo”. (lettera di Elio Zorzi a Paola Masino, 16 Ottobre 1941)
Ad essere franco mi sembra che le Memorie prime potrebbero essere soppresse senza danno alcuno per l’armonia del romanzo. Quanto alle Memorie seconde, bisognerebbe tagliare tutto quello che si riferisce più o meno indirettamente alla guerra. Poi, quà e là, altri pezzi che Le segnalerò alla sua venuta a Milano.
Sono veramente dolente di dover fare la parte del censore con un’opera che ha una sua unità stilistica e spirituale e un tono altissimo, e che perciò non è, a mio parere, adatta a figurare nell’appendice di un giornale che si rivolge a un pubblico vastissimo. (lettera di Elio Zorzi a Paola Masino, 29 ottobre 1941)

Hence, two factors can be said to have shaped the version of the text that was published on Tempo: on the one hand, the difficulty of the writing compared to the periodical’s target audience; on the other hand, clearly, the problematic of the lines mentioning the war that are mainly cynical and unaligned with the patriotic fascist propaganda.

A third kind of corrections, as mentioned in the authorial commentary at the end of the ’45 edition, seems to surround the theme of religion, as we learn from Masino’s authorial note to the 1945 edition:
“Non se ne proibiva la pubblicazione, ma s’impose che ne fossero soppressi alcuni episodi, nonché tutte le citazioni dall’Antico Testamento;”

In the section of the novel we decided to edit, this is particularly evident: a section of the Housewife’s journal mentions Sundays and was extended and expanded in the ‘45 edition, compared to the ’41. A whole journal entry was added in the 1945 edition:
Lunedì 3 – Tra poco sarà Pasqua, e tuttavia intorno ci sarà ancora la guerra. La Pasqua non può oramai più non scoccare e forse in quell’ora la guerra si cancella fino a Caino, cui tutti gli uomini guardano con pietà e rispetto. Ma penso alle guerre mai sorprese dalla Pasqua, quando Caino era ancora senza scampo maledetto.

These structural changes that were made by the author carried with them also some important semantic meaning, exactly concerning these three conceptual areas. The most evident to us is the rearrangement of the Memorie prime section, that were also subject of discussion with the editor of the periodical in 1941:

Most of the content stayed the same but in the 41 version, the meditations of the Housewife are embedded in precise dates as proper journal entries, whereas in the 45, the same content is structured as a whole block of text, almost monologue-like. Moreover, the text is conceptually divided in three sections by the addition of a title-like brief sentence where the Housewife addresses herself as “woman”:
Salve a me, donna; e ascòltati.
Parliamo ancora: ma oggi io ascolterò e tu dirai.
Pensiamo insieme.

Considering that the author herself considers the novel to be a “woman’s portrait” and knowing that, alongside the plot, the Housewife evolves, not accordingly to some inherent personal characteristics but in relation to the “female role” that she is supposed to cover to fit in societal standards. This addition – in the only chapter where the character is portrayed on her own, living independently in the Capital city – is a small but major addition: These sentences suggest a progressive increase of personal awareness of the character as she reflects on womanhood and conceives somehow a philosophy of birth and death (knowing the title of the novel, we can’t possibly avoid thinking that this passage is truly to be considered a key passage in the novel, a fact that the censor did not understand at all, considering it useless to the narration and too difficult!).

It is not surprising that Masino did not accept to cut it altogether from the narrative, as the censor suggested, but ended up compromising with a journal-like camouflage that could seem to soften up the difficulty of the content.
