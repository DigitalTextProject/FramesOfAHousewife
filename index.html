About

Frames of a Housewife's goal is to inquire into the troubled creative process behind Paola Masino's most famous book Nascita e morte della Massaia, highlighting - through a workflow of fragmentations, recompositions and interconnections that is distinctive to the digital environment - how the different voices of the censor, the author, the characters and the editor all concurred to the peculiar nature of the novel.
